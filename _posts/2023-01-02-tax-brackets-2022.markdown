The tax brackets for 2022 for single filers are:

| Taxable Income    | Tax Rate |
|-------------------|----------|
| Up to $10,275     | 10%      |
| $10,276-$41,775   | 12%      |
| $41,776-$89,075   | 22%      |
| $89,076-$170,050  | 24%      |
| $170,051-$215,950 | 32%      |
| $215,951-$539,900 | 35%      |
| Over $539,900     | 37%      |
