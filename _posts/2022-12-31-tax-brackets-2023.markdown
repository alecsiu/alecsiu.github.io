The tax brackets for 2023 for single filers are:

| Taxable Income    | Tax Rate |
|-------------------|----------|
| Up to $11,000     | 10%      |
| $11,001-$44,725   | 12%      |
| $44,726-$95,375   | 22%      |
| $95,376-$182,100  | 24%      |
| $182,101-$231,250 | 32%      |
| $231,250-$578,125 | 35%      |
| Over $578,125     | 37%      |
